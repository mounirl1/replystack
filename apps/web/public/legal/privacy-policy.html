Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 15, 2026
Last Updated: January 7, 2026

ReplyStack ("we", "our", "us") operates the ReplyStack browser extension and web application (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Introduction

ReplyStack is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data. This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, password (hashed using industry-standard encryption)
  • Business Information: Location names, platform credentials (encrypted at rest)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Stripe (we only store your Stripe Customer ID)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Review Content: Reviews you choose to generate responses for (stored temporarily for processing)
  • Generated Responses: AI-generated replies and their metadata (tone, language, tokens used)
  • Usage Data: Number of replies generated, platforms used, quota consumption

2.3 Browser Extension Data

  • Authentication Token: Stored locally in chrome.storage.local (not accessible to other extensions)
  • User Preferences: Language, default tone, theme settings

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Generate AI-powered review responses
  • Authenticate: Securely identify you across extension and web application
  • Billing: Process subscription payments via Stripe
  • Improve: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Support: Respond to your inquiries and resolve technical issues

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your reviews for marketing purposes without explicit consent
  • Share your data except as described in Section 4
  • Train AI models on your review content

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your data only with the following trusted service providers:

4.1 Service Providers

AI Service Provider

  • Purpose: Core functionality - AI reply generation
  • Data Shared: Review text, rating, platform (no personal identifiers)
  • Location: United States (secure third-party AI servers)
  • Security: Enterprise-grade encryption and data protection

Stripe

  • Purpose: Subscription billing and payment processing
  • Data Shared: Email, payment details (fully managed by Stripe)
  • Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

4.2 Legal Obligations

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process, court orders, or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Location

  • Database: Hosted on secure cloud servers in Europe (EU)
  • Extension Data: Stored locally on your device (chrome.storage.local)
  • Backups: Encrypted daily backups retained for 30 days

5.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS)
  • Encryption at Rest: OAuth tokens encrypted using Laravel encrypted casts
  • Authentication: Laravel Sanctum tokens with automatic expiration
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions and IP whitelisting for sensitive operations
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability scanning

5.3 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: All personal data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Generated Responses: Retained for historical purposes (deletable on request)
  • Review Content: Deleted within 30 days after response generation

6. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format (JSON)
  • Object: Opt-out of certain data processing activities
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data in specific circumstances
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke previously given consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@reply-stack.app

We will respond to all requests within 30 days as required by GDPR.

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Essential Cookies

  • Preferences: Language, theme, and interface settings

7.2 Analytics

We use privacy-friendly analytics (Plausible) that:

  • Do NOT collect personal information

7.3 Opting Out
